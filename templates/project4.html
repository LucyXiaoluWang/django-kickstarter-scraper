Charles Josef Spring 2013 Fashion Collection

This project is to raise the funds for fabrics to produce samples of my fashion line for the Spring 2013 season to sell in stores

  • Funding ended: Sep 16, 2012

  • Don't want to forget? Click the star to add this project to your profile.

    Remind Me

This project is to raise the funds to produce samples of my fashion line for the Spring 2013 season. I am covering most of the cost myself, the amount being raised here covers about a third and will provide funding to purchase the fine textiles for the project. I will be doing the design, pattern making, and sample making in my studio The Line will be a collection of luxury garments mostly catering to the mother of the Bride and Groom which is a segment that is lacking in the market. My years of designing these garments for private clients has given me the formula I need to offer these goods in the retail market and move my business to the next level.  Charles Josef Designs is an environmentally responsible business that utilizes eco friendly and fair trade textiles to produce fine garments that provide years of wear.  Additionally we have developed a "Waste Not" program where remnants are used to make useful gift items and thus reduces our production waste by 90%.   Our studio utilizes the local work force to produce quality goods that are made in beautiful Asheville, NC, USA.  This is just the begining of our journey to expand our business and to reestablish production jobs in Western North Carolina that will not only offer employment opportunites for skilled artisans but honor their skills and hard work with wages that allow them to maintain the comfortable life that they are worthy of.  Thank You in advance for supporting our vision to use our creative expression to touch people's life in many positive ways.
